About The Void

This is a safe, anonymous corner of the internet.

Our mission is to provide a space where you can share your deepest thoughts, regrets, or funny stories without anyone knowing who you are.

How it Works

  • Anonymity: Every time you enter, you get a new funny identity. No accounts, no passwords.
  • AI Moderation: We use Google Gemini AI to ensure the wall stays free from hate speech.
  • Control: You can delete your own confessions during your session.
Tech Stack

Backend: Flask (Python) | Database: SQLite | AI: Google Gemini | UI: Bootstrap & Animate.css
